About Us

SafeHerd was born from the vision of a Rwandan student, Armel Munyaneza, who witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of livestock theft on rural communities. Determined to make a difference, Armel developed an innovative solution combining GPS tracking with smart technology to protect livestock and empower farmers. SafeHerd utilizes solar-powered GPS trackers that function with or without network coverage, providing real-time location data and instant alerts through a user-friendly mobile app. This innovative approach not only alarms theft but also helps farmers manage their herds more effectively, ensuring their livelihoods are secure.
